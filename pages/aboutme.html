About Me

Hello there! I am Daysen Gyatt, a Computational Media student at the Georgia Institute of Technology. As an aspiring professional in the field, I am deeply committed to the intersection of technology and creativity. My multifaceted skill set encompasses roles as a maker, creator, designer, and storyteller, with a diverse portfolio reflecting my proficiency across various creative mediums.

Born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia, my formative years were influenced by a rich environment surrounded by musicians, artists, and builders. This upbringing instilled in me a profound appreciation for the arts and a drive to channel my creative energy into meaningful projects. Through academic pursuits and personal endeavors, I continuously seek to hone my craft and contribute innovative solutions within the realm of Computational Media.

Thank you for visiting my digital portfolio, where you can explore a curated collection of my work and witness the fusion of technical expertise and artistic expression. I invite you to delve into the diverse range of projects that showcase my dedication to excellence and passion for pushing the boundaries of digital media.
